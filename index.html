How we support a small test

We do not start with a full catalog. We start with a short reference board, supplier feasibility, and the risk points that affect real orders.

1. Reference board

Build a 3-5 style direction based on your current brand look.

2. Supplier check

Review material, plating route, MOQ, sample fee, and lead time.

3. Sample follow-up

Track photos, stone setting, finishing, ear post details, and packaging.

4. Bulk readiness

Compare approved samples with bulk photos before shipment decisions.

Best fit

Good fit

Not a fit

  • One-off end-customer customization or repair work.
  • Requests for unverified waterproof, hypoallergenic, or compliance claims.
  • Large catalog scraping, dropship automation, or lowest-price-only sourcing.

Compliance note: we can help request supplier declarations, product details, and test documents when available, but we do not certify waterproof, hypoallergenic, nickel-free, lead-free, or regulatory claims ourselves.
