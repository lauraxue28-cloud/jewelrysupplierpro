What we check

We do not send a giant catalog and ask you to choose blindly. We start with a short reference direction, then check supplier feasibility and the risk points that affect real sample orders.

1. Reference board

Build a 3-5 style direction based on your current brand look and price band.

2. Supplier check

Review base material, plating route, MOQ, sample fee, sample time, and bulk lead time.

3. Sample follow-up

Track product photos, stone setting, finishing, clasp, ear post details, and packaging.

4. Bulk readiness

Compare approved samples with bulk pre-shipment photos before release decisions.

Best fit

Good fit

  • Boutique jewelry stores testing new gold-tone huggies.
  • DTC brands that need China-side sample, photo, and packaging follow-up.
  • Teams that want a small reference board before requesting final quotes.

Not a fit

  • One-off end-customer customization or repair work.
  • Requests for unverified waterproof, hypoallergenic, or compliance claims.
  • Large catalog scraping, dropship automation, or lowest-price-only purchasing.

Compliance note: we can help request supplier declarations, product details, and test documents when available, but we do not certify waterproof, hypoallergenic, nickel-free, lead-free, or regulatory claims ourselves.
